Contact DBS

If any of the information on your DBS Online Result is incorrect

You need to raise a dispute with DBS. This includes any:

  • incorrect personal details
  • incorrect conviction information
  • other discrepancies

You can read the read the DBS certificate dispute and fingerprint consent form guidance (opens in a new tab) for more detailed information.

By email

RaiseDispute@dbs.gov.uk

By phone

03000 200 190

Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm (excluding public holidays)

Please read the call charges guidance (opens in a new tab) to find out how much this should cost.
