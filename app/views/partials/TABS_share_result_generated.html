Your share code is:

  • single use
  • case sensitive
  • only valid for 21 days

What you need to do now

You need to:

  • send the email we have just sent you, to the person you want to see your result
  • give the share code to the person you want to see your result

You can request another email if you have not received the email or you cannot find it. For your security you must send the share code separately to the email, for example by text message.
