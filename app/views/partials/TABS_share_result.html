Sharing your DBS Online Result

You can use this part of the service to share your result with someone else, like your employer.

How this works

After you tell us who you are sharing your result with, you will be able to generate a one-time share code. When you generate the share code, we will send you an email with:

  • full instructions
  • the last 6 digits of your certificate number
  • a link to access your result

The person you send these to will be able to use them to view your result.
