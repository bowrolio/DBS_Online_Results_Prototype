Dispute Result

Contact us if you think your DBS certificate is wrong, including:

  • incorrect personal details
  • incorrect conviction information, or
  • other discrepancies

If you receive a DBS certificate with a conviction that doesn't relate to you, you should also inform the person who requested that you complete the form as soon as possible.
