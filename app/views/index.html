Viewing or sharing your result

You can use this service to:

  • view your result
  • share your result with someone else, like an employer
  • cancel any unused share codes that have not expired

To use this service you will need to sign in to your GOV.​UK One Login. If you have more than one application you will also need your application reference number.

You can go to the DBS Online Result service to view or share your result.

DBS Update Service

You can register for the DBS Update Service using your application reference number.

The DBS Update Service allows you to keep your DBS check up to date. It costs £16 a year. There is no cost if you are a volunteer.
