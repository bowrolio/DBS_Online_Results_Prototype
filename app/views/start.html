View your DBS Online Result

You can use this service to:

  • view your DBS Online Result
  • manage sharing your DBS Online Result
  • contact us if you believe any information on your DBS Online result is incorrect

What you will need

To view your DBS Online Result, you will need:

  • to sign into the GOV.UK One Login you provided when making your application
  • your application number, which we sent to you by email. You will only need this if you have more than one application in progress
