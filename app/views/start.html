View your DBS Online Result

You can use this service to:

  • view your DBS Online Result
  • share your result with someone else, like an employer
  • find out how to contact us if you believe any information on your result is incorrect

Viewing your result

To view your result you will need to sign in to the GOV.UK One Login you created when you applied.

If you have more than one application you will also need your application number, which we have emailed to you.
