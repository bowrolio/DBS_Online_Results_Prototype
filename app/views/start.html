View your DBS Online Result

You can use this service to:

  • view your DBS Online Result
  • manage sharing your DBS Online Result
  • contact us if you believe any information on your DBS Online result is incorrect

What you will need

To view your results, you will need your:

  • security code, which we will send to you by text
  • the application number, which we sent to you in the email
  • GOV.UK account login details. You will be able to create an account if you do not already have one
