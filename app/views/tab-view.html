Share your DBS Online Result

You can share your DBS Online Result with someone else, like your employer.

How to share your DBS Online Result

To share your DBS Online Result with someone else, you will need to send them:

  • a share code, which will be generated on the next screen
  • the last 6 digits of your certificate number
  • a link: https://www.view-criminal-record-check.service.gov.uk/view-result/employer

When you generate a share code you will receive an email containing the last 6 digits of your certificate number and the web link.
